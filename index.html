Pet Concierge Service

THE FURRY TAIL Pet Services offers personalized pet care in the comfort and convenience of your own home or at our home daycare on special cases. If you’re anything like us, you have an incredible connection with your dogs and cats. When you’re out of town, you want to make sure they’re with someone who will love them just as much as you do.

Be at peace knowing that while you’re away, your pet is receiving the best in care, love, attention, and affection. The goal is that while you’re away, your pet will feel at home, happy and comfortable. We are dedicated to bonding with pets and building positive relationships with clients. If you’re looking for professional, reliable and personalized pet care, you’ve found the right place!
